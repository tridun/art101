Introduction

Hi, my name is Tristan Dunlop and I'm a third year AGPM major at UCSC. Prior to ART 101, I have had some programming experience with C and Java, but none when it comes to HTML and CSS. To help experiment and encourage myself to learn more, I decided that it would be best to make this site a small digital portfolio of my art work.

Experience

From an early age, I've played around with a lot of different mediums, from pencil sketching to acrylic painting, but in recent years, I have experimented with more non-traditional art forms such as duct-tape, spray paint, welding, and digital art.
